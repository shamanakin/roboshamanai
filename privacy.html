Privacy

What I Collect

When you use the contact form on this site, I collect only what you provide: your name, email address, the type of service you're interested in, and your message. That's it.

How I Use It

I use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. Your email goes directly to my inbox and is stored there — not in a database, not in a CRM, not on a marketing list. I read it, I reply to it, and that's the end of the data flow.

What I Don't Do

  • I don't sell or share your information with anyone.
  • I don't add you to any newsletter or mailing list.
  • I don't use tracking pixels or aggressive analytics.
  • I don't store your payment details (payments are handled via PayPal/Venmo).

Questions or Deletion Requests

If you want me to delete any information you've sent, or if you have any questions about how your data is handled, just email me at matthew@roboshamanai.com.
