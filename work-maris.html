Client case study

Maris Dreaming
From standing start to real reach

A therapist-artist with the creative work done — but no storefront, no funnel, no system. I built the pipeline in two weeks; she was running it solo by week four.

The situation

Maris Loeffler is a therapist and AI-artist selling surreal digital downloads on Etsy (marisdreaming). She had the art and the audience instinct — not the shop architecture, SEO, Pinterest→Etsy funnel, or automation layer. That's the blocker I see constantly in this niche: the creative work exists; the go-to-market machinery doesn't.

What I built

  • Etsy shop end-to-end: 94 listings, 15 collections, SEO titles/tags, banner, policies, launch promo
  • Pinterest → Etsy and Instagram → Etsy discovery funnel (@marisdreaming)
  • Automation and sync workflows so she could focus on making art, not admin
  • Brand guide, pin guides, and platform-specific field entries for handoff

Timeline: built in two weeks → coached handoff on week three → Maris running it solo by week four. She makes the art; the machinery is mine.

Results (verified 2026-05-30)

74.7K Pinterest impressions / 30d
587 Saves / 30d
94 Listings published
5.0★ First reviews (5)

Shop is ~2 months old with 10 early sales. The hard part for a new shop — real reach from zero — is clearly working; sales are early and climbing. (Pinterest's growth figure maxes out at >999% against a near-zero prior month — the absolute numbers above are the story.)

Method

Same pattern I use on every project — mapped to Maris:

Discovery — What actually blocks you? (Here: art ready, no GTM system.)

Build — Minimum viable shop + funnel that fits how she works.

Ship — Live storefront, real listings, real pins — not a deck.

Hand off — Week three coaching; week four she's running it solo.

I'm listed on her Etsy shop as Consultant/Developer — shop setup, SEO, integrations, and automation. Third-party verifiable; no inflated claims.
